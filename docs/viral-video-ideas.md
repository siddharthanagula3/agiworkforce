## Hook Frameworks That Work

These are the proven hook patterns backed by analysis of thousands of viral short-form videos:

### 1. Internal Contradiction

Present two opposing states. The brain hates unresolved tension and stops scrolling.

> "I have 66 AI models on my desktop and I pay $0/month for the app."

### 2. Curiosity Gap

Open a knowledge gap that demands resolution.

> "There's a free app that does everything ChatGPT does, plus 65 other models."

### 3. Contrarian / Hot Take

Bold statement that challenges conventional wisdom.

> "ChatGPT is the worst way to use AI in 2026."

### 4. Problem-Solution Pair

Name a specific pain point, then smash-cut to the fix.

> "Switching between 5 AI tabs is killing your productivity."

### 5. Specificity Anchor

Concrete numbers create instant credibility.

> "I tested 66 AI models in one app. Here's the only one you need."

### 6. POV / Identity

Viewer self-inserts into the scenario.

> "POV: You just discovered you can run AI agents that control your entire desktop."

### 7. Pattern Interrupt

Visual or conceptual jolt that breaks the scroll.

> [Screen recording of AI autonomously browsing, typing, clicking] -- no narration for 2 seconds, then: "This isn't a human."

## Production Playbook

### Recording Setup

- **Screen recordings**: Use the app itself at 9:16 crop. OBS or built-in macOS screen recording. No watermarks.
- **Talking head + screen**: Picture-in-picture with face cam in corner. Shows authentic human + product.
- **Captions**: Always. 85% of TikTok is watched without sound. Use CapCut auto-captions or Captions app.
- **Music**: Trending sounds boost reach, but avoid drowning out narration. Use as background at 15-20% volume.

### Filming Principles

1. **Raw over polished** -- Authentic content performs 60% better than overproduced content. Screen recordings with genuine narration beat studio setups.
2. **No intros** -- Start with the hook. Zero "Hey guys, welcome back to my channel."
3. **Jump cuts** -- Keep visual pacing fast. Cut every 2-3 seconds during explanations.
4. **Text overlays** -- Reinforce every key point with on-screen text. Different color for emphasis.
5. **Vertical only** -- Never letterbox a horizontal recording. Always record or crop to 9:16.

### Publishing Cadence

- **Minimum**: 4 videos/week across platforms
- **Optimal**: 1 video/day, cross-posted to all 3 platforms with minor tweaks
- **Batch production**: Record 5-7 videos in one session. Edit and schedule across the week.
- **A/B test hooks**: Record 2 versions of the same content with different hooks. Post the better performer.

### Hashtag Strategy

Primary: #AItools #AIagent #AIproductivity #FreeAI #AGIWorkforce
Secondary: #TechTips #ProductivityHack #WorkSmarter #AIautomation #DesktopAI
Niche: #BYOK #MCPtools #LocalAI #AIprivacy #AIagents

### Engagement Tactics

- **Pin a comment** with the download link on every video
- **Reply to every comment** in the first hour (signals engagement to algorithm)
- **Ask questions** in captions to drive comments: "Which model would you pick?" / "Would you trust AI with this?"
- **Create series**: "66 Models in 66 Days" -- one model review per day builds followership
- **Duet/stitch bait**: Make claims that invite responses. Controversial takes get stitched.
