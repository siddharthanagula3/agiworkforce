## 1. What Does This App Do?

### The Big Picture

AGI Workforce is like having a super-smart assistant that lives on your computer. You talk to it in plain English, and it does things for you automatically.

**Example conversations:**

- "Find all the invoices in my email from last month and put them in a folder"
- "Schedule a meeting with John next Tuesday at 2pm"
- "Write a summary of this document"
- "Create a spreadsheet with my sales data"
- "Search the web for the best coffee shops near me"

AGI Workforce figures out the steps needed and does them without asking you for permission at every step. If it makes a mistake, you can say "undo that" and it reverses everything.

### What Makes It Different?

| Traditional Software                      | AGI Workforce                 |
| ----------------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Click buttons, fill forms, navigate menus | Just describe what you want   |
| You do each step manually                 | AI does steps automatically   |
| Mistakes require manual fixing            | One-click undo for everything |
| Need to learn each app                    | Just talk naturally           |

## 5. Safety & Control

### The Undo System

**Everything AGI Workforce does can be reversed.**

If AGI Workforce:

- Creates a file → You can delete it
- Deletes a file → You can restore it
- Sends an email → (Draft only unless you approve)
- Changes a document → You can revert to the previous version

Just say "undo" or click the undo button.

### Approval System

For sensitive actions, AGI Workforce asks for your permission first:

**Auto-approved (safe actions):**

- Reading files
- Searching the web
- Looking at your calendar
- Drafting messages (not sending)

**Requires your approval:**

- Sending emails
- Posting to social media
- Making purchases
- Deleting files permanently
- Changing account settings

You'll see a popup asking "Allow this action?" with details of what will happen.

### Folder Permissions

You control exactly which folders AGI Workforce can access:

1. Go to Settings → Allowed Directories
2. Add folders you want AGI Workforce to access
3. AGI Workforce cannot see anything outside these folders

**Recommended:** Add only the folders you need, like "Documents" or a specific project folder.

### Resource Limits

AGI Workforce has built-in limits to prevent runaway operations:

- **Time limit:** Tasks stop after 5 minutes of continuous work
- **Step limit:** Maximum 1,000 steps per task
- **Memory limit:** Won't use more than 2GB of RAM
- **Retry limit:** Gives up after 3 consecutive failures

### Prompt Injection Protection

If you're browsing a website that tries to trick AGI Workforce with hidden instructions, it detects this and ignores the malicious commands. It only follows YOUR instructions.

## 8. Multi-Device Sync

### Pairing Your Devices

Use AGI Workforce on multiple devices that stay in sync:

1. **On your desktop:** Go to Settings → Devices → "Pair New Device"
2. **A 6-digit code appears** (like "ABC123")
3. **On your phone/tablet:** Open the web app and enter the code
4. **Devices are now paired!**

Codes expire after 5 minutes for security.

### What Syncs

- Conversation history
- Settings and preferences
- Connected apps
- Credit balance

### What Doesn't Sync

- Files on your local computer
- Browser sessions
- Desktop automation controls

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. Your files stay on your computer. AGI Workforce only sees what you show it. Conversations can be deleted anytime.

**Q: Does AGI Workforce learn from my data?**
A: AGI Workforce learns patterns within your session to help you better, but your data is not used to train the underlying AI models.

**Q: Can AGI Workforce access anything on my computer?**
A: No. You must explicitly grant access to specific folders. AGI Workforce cannot see anything outside those folders.

**Q: What if I don't have internet?**
A: The desktop app requires internet to use AI features. Some offline features (like viewing past conversations) may work.

### Safety Questions

**Q: Can AGI Workforce make irreversible mistakes?**
A: Almost all actions can be undone. For truly irreversible actions (like sending an email), AGI Workforce asks for approval first.

**Q: What if a website tries to trick AGI Workforce?**
A: AGI Workforce has built-in protection against "prompt injection" attacks. It ignores hidden commands on websites.

**Q: Can AGI Workforce spend my money?**
A: No. Any purchase-related actions require explicit approval, and AGI Workforce cannot access your payment methods directly.

### Technical Questions

**Q: How does AGI Workforce "see" my screen?**
A: When you enable screen sharing, AGI Workforce takes screenshots to understand what's on your screen. You control when this happens.

**Q: What AI models are used?**
A: AGI Workforce uses multiple AI providers (Claude, GPT, Gemini, and others) and automatically picks the best one for each task.

**Q: Can I use my own AI API keys?**
A: Yes, advanced users can configure their own API keys in settings.

### Billing Questions

**Q: What happens if I run out of credits mid-task?**
A: The current task will complete, but you won't be able to start new tasks until you have more credits.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Contact support for refund requests. We handle them on a case-by-case basis.

**Q: Do unused credits roll over?**
A: No, credits reset each billing cycle.
