## 2. Viral Pattern Analysis

### 2.1 Top Performing Hook Categories

**RANK 1 — SHOCK + IMPOSSIBLE DEMO** (highest views)

- Formula: `"[Product] just did something that shouldn't be possible"`
- Example: "@maverickgpt: AI is actually getting out of hand…😳 — This person took a photo of the ground and said 'I dropped my ring can you help me find it?' ChatGPT then drew a circle around exactly where the ring was."
- Views: 1.3M+
- Why it works: Proof > promise. Viewers re-share because they want friends to see "can you believe this?"

**RANK 2 — COMPETITOR SWITCH STORY** (high comments/shares)

- Formula: `"Goodbye [competitor], I'm switching to [X] because…"`
- Example: "@nathanespinoza: Goodbye ChatGPT, it was actually good knowing you… here's how to switch to Claude"
- Views: 105K, but 384 SHARES — very high share ratio
- Why it works: Debate/controversy triggers comments ("no don't switch!" "you're right"). Algorithm loves comments.

**RANK 3 — SECRET/HACK REVEAL** (saves + shares)

- Formula: `"ChatGPT is [scary secret] — here's how to protect yourself in 30 seconds"`
- Example: "@maverickgpt: ChatGPT is reading your conversations and sending them to the police 😳"
- Views: 125K
- Why it works: Fear + empowerment = saves. Saves signal long-term interest to algorithm.

**RANK 4 — RELATABLE HUMOR** (massive reach, low effort)

- Formula: Funny screenshot or reaction to AI saying something absurd
- Example: @vibestealer — 9.8M views, no effort content
- Why it works: Pure share bait. Hard to replicate for product demos, but humor variants exist.

**RANK 5 — PROMPT/HACK LIST** (saves, DM CTAs)

- Formula: `"Screenshot this ChatGPT prompt right now"` + DM CTA
- Example: @sabrina_ramonov — "Wealth Maximization Mode prompt… Comment 'MONEY' and I'll send it"
- Why it works: DM CTAs build audience lists. Saves accumulate over time.

### 2.2 Hook Word/Phrase Patterns

**Opening lines that WORK:**

- "AI is actually getting out of hand…😳"
- "I can't believe what just happened"
- "Goodbye [X], I'm switching to [Y]"
- "Why does no one talk about this?"
- "Screenshot this right now"
- "ChatGPT just did something that shouldn't be possible"
- "Stop using [tool] like a search engine"
- "This one AI tool just saved me [X hours/dollars]"
- "POV: You just discovered [X]"

**Opening lines that FAIL for product content:**

- "Introducing [product name]…" — sounds like an ad, scrolled past
- "Check out our new feature…" — corporate, boring
- "[Product] is now available…" — PR speak, zero emotion

### 2.3 Video Structure Formula (60-second format)

Based on all top-performing content:

```
[0-3s]   HOOK — One punchy line or visual shock. No intro. No "hey guys".
[3-10s]  PAIN POINT or CONTEXT — Why the viewer needs this RIGHT NOW
[10-35s] DEMO or PROOF — Show it actually working, no fluff
[35-50s] AMPLIFY — "And here's the part that blew my mind…"
[50-57s] CTA — Single, simple action
[57-60s] LOOP BAIT — End on something that makes them re-watch
```

### 2.4 Caption Patterns

Best-performing captions:

1. Hook line = first caption sentence (appears as preview text)
2. Numbered steps or bullet points if educational
3. DM/Comment CTA ("Comment 'AI' and I'll send it")
4. 3-5 relevant hashtags only (not 30)
5. End with a question to drive comments

### 2.6 Algorithm Signals That Matter

From platform data:

- **Shares > Likes** for algorithmic push (shares = content quality)
- **Comments** signal controversy/emotion — drives distribution
- **Saves** signal long-term value — great for Reels/Shorts
- **Watch-through rate** is the #1 signal — hook must hold first 3s

## 4. Content Strategy Recommendations

### Pillar 1: Mind-Blowing Demo Videos (shock + impossible demo)

Target: All audiences | Goal: Shares, new reach

- Show AGI Workforce doing something no single AI can do
- Visuals: Split screen of 5 models answering same question

### Pillar 2: Competitor Switch Videos (controversy + story)

Target: ChatGPT/Claude users | Goal: Comments, debate

- "I cancelled ChatGPT + Claude + Gemini and pay $0 now"
- Hook into existing switch conversation

### Pillar 3: Productivity Hack Videos (saves + DM CTA)

Target: Freelancers, business users | Goal: Saves, email list

- "5 AI automations that make me $X/hour"
- "How I review 50 emails in 3 minutes with AI"

### Pillar 4: Dev/Builder Videos (GitHub stars + community)

Target: Developers | Goal: Stars, contributors

- "I built an open AI desktop app that beats Claude Desktop"
- Show the code, show the stack, show it working

### Pillar 5: Mobile Feature Videos (UNIQUE differentiator)

Target: All | Goal: Virality from novelty

- "I approved an AI task from my phone while driving"
- This is UNIQUE — no competitor has this. Very shareable.
