## Pricing Philosophy

### Core Principles

1. **Value-Based Pricing:** Price based on value delivered (time saved), not cost to serve
2. **Progressive Disclosure:** Start free, expand as users discover value
3. **Fair Use:** Generous limits for real users, prevent abuse
4. **Transparent:** No hidden fees, clear limits, predictable costs
5. **Flexible:** Monthly or annual, pay-as-you-go credits available

### Pricing Objectives

1. **Maximize LTV:** Higher tier users stay longer and expand
2. **Optimize Conversion:** Remove friction in upgrade path
3. **Enable Self-Service:** Automated billing, instant activation
4. **Support Enterprise:** Custom pricing for large deals
5. **Fund Growth:** Sustainable unit economics (LTV:CAC > 3:1)

## Usage Limits

### Fair Use Policy

**Definition:** Unlimited tiers have "fair use" limits to prevent abuse while allowing legitimate heavy usage.

**Fair Use Thresholds:**

- Pro: 10,000 AI messages/month (330/day)
- Max: 100,000 AI messages/month (3,300/day)
- Enterprise: Custom

**Actions on Fair Use Violations:**

1. Email notification at 80% of fair use
2. Warning email at 100% of fair use
3. Temporary rate limiting at 150% of fair use
4. Upgrade recommendation
5. Account review at 200% of fair use

**Legitimate Heavy Use Examples:**

- Large codebase refactoring
- Batch processing scripts
- API usage from CI/CD pipelines
- Team usage across multiple time zones

**Abuse Indicators:**

- Reselling API access
- Running public services on our API
- Cryptocurrency mining
- Botnet activities

### Rate Limits

## Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring Strategy

**Price Anchors:**

1. **Enterprise** (highest) - Makes Max look affordable
2. **Max** ($300) - Makes Pro look like great value
3. **Pro** ($30) - Primary conversion target
4. **Hobby** ($10) - Low-risk first paid tier
5. **Free** - Acquisition engine

### Value Communication

**Don't Say:** "AI automation platform for $30/month"
**Do Say:** "Save 20 hours/week (worth $2,000) for just $30/month - 65x ROI"

**Framing Techniques:**

1. **Time Saved:** "Save 15 hours/week"
2. **Money Saved:** "Worth $1,500/month in saved developer time"
3. **Productivity Gain:** "Ship features 3x faster"
4. **ROI:** "50-100x return on investment"
5. **Opportunity Cost:** "What else could you build with 20 extra hours?"

### Price Sensitivity Segments

**Price Insensitive (Target: Pro/Max):**

- Professional developers (expense to employer)
- Agencies billing clients
- Well-funded startups
- Enterprise developers

**Price Sensitive (Target: Hobby):**

- Students
- Independent developers
- Side project builders
- Emerging markets

**Procurement-Driven (Target: Enterprise):**

- Large companies
- Government agencies
- Regulated industries

### Psychological Triggers

**Scarcity:** "Limited beta access" (launch phase only)
**Social Proof:** "Join 100,000 developers"
**Authority:** "Used by teams at Google, Microsoft, Anthropic"
**Reciprocity:** Generous free tier creates obligation
**Consistency:** Annual discounts reward commitment

## Pricing Evolution

### Phase 1: Launch (Q1-Q2 2026)

**Current Pricing:**

- Free: $0
- Hobby: $10/mo
- Pro: $30/mo
- Max: $300/mo
- Enterprise: Custom

**Rationale:**

- Penetration pricing to gain market share
- Compete with Claude Cowork launch
- Build user base quickly
- Establish competitive position

### Phase 2: Growth (Q3-Q4 2026)

**Potential Changes:**

- Grandfather existing users at current pricing
- New pricing for new users:
  - Hobby: $15/mo (+50%)
  - Pro: $40/mo (+33%)
  - Max: $400/mo (+33%)

**Trigger:**

- Reach 50,000 paying customers
- Achieve product-market fit
- Enhanced feature set warrants increase

### Phase 3: Maturity (2027+)

**Potential Changes:**

- Premium tier between Pro and Max ($100/mo)
- Usage-based pricing option
- Industry-specific pricing
- Regional pricing for emerging markets

**Rationale:**

- More granular tiers capture more value
- Usage-based for predictable heavy users
- Maximize revenue per customer

### Grandfathering Policy

**Existing Customers:**

- Keep current pricing forever (if continuous subscription)
- Lock in annual pricing on renewal
- Receive new features at old price

**Why Grandfather:**

- Rewards early adopters
- Reduces churn during price increases
- Builds goodwill and loyalty
- Word-of-mouth marketing

## Pricing FAQs

**Q: Why is Free tier so generous?**
A: Acquisition engine. Free users refer others, provide feedback, and convert to paid over time. Cost to serve is low ($0.50/user).

**Q: Can I change tiers anytime?**
A: Yes, upgrade immediately. Downgrade takes effect at next billing cycle to prevent abuse.

**Q: What happens if I exceed fair use?**
A: We'll notify you and recommend upgrade or purchasing credits. We never cut off access without warning.

**Q: Do you offer non-profit discounts?**
A: Yes, 30% discount for 501(c)(3) organizations and educational institutions.

**Q: Is there a student discount?**
A: Students get free access to Hobby tier with valid .edu email.

**Q: Can I pay annually?**
A: Yes, save 17% with annual billing on Hobby, Pro, and Max tiers.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit card (Stripe) for self-serve. Wire transfer and invoicing for Enterprise.

**Q: Do credits roll over?**
A: Plan limits reset monthly. Purchased credits valid for 12 months.
