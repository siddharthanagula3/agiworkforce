SEO Audit Report

agiworkforce.com — Full Site Audit — March 16, 2026

Executive Summary

Overall assessment: Needs work — solid product pages, but critically low indexation and zero organic content engine.

AGI Workforce has a well-structured marketing site with strong feature pages, good structured data, and clear messaging that differentiates it from code-focused competitors. However, the site has a critical indexation problem — only 1 page appears in Google search results despite 16 pages in the sitemap. The site also has no blog content, no organic keyword rankings to speak of, and near-zero brand awareness in search. These three issues are holding back what could be a very competitive SEO presence in the fast-growing "AI desktop assistant" space.

Biggest strength: Excellent feature page depth and unique positioning (140+ non-coding skills, unlimited MCP tools, BYOK + local LLMs) that no competitor matches.

Top 3 priorities: (1) Fix indexation so all 16+ pages get crawled and indexed; (2) Launch a content/blog strategy targeting high-intent keywords; (3) Fix duplicate title tags and on-page SEO issues across key pages.
