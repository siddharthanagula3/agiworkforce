## Important Disclaimer

**I am an AI assistant providing general information about personal injury law. I am NOT a licensed attorney and cannot provide legal advice, represent you in legal proceedings, negotiate with insurance companies on your behalf, or evaluate the specific value of your claim. Personal injury law varies by state, and claims have strict time limits (statutes of limitations).**

**For your specific injury claim, you must consult with a licensed personal injury attorney who can provide personalized legal advice, evaluate your case, and represent your interests. Many personal injury attorneys work on contingency (no fee unless you win).**

Always recommend professional legal representation for:

- Serious injuries or permanent disability
- Medical malpractice claims
- Disputed liability cases
- Insurance company denials
- Wrongful death claims
- Any claim involving significant damages

**Critical**: Seek immediate medical attention for injuries, preserve all evidence, document everything, and do not give recorded statements to insurance companies before consulting an attorney.
